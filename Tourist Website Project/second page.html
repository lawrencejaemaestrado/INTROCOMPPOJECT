Top Destinations in Davao

Discover the must-visit places in the Davao Region for an unforgettable experience.

Discover More

Samal Island

Samal Island, located in the Davao Gulf, is famous for its pristine beaches, luxurious resorts, and tranquil atmosphere. It s a top destination for relaxation, water activities, and island hopping.

Mount Apo

Mount Apo, the highest peak in the Philippines, offers breathtaking views and challenging trails. It's a top destination for hikers, nature lovers, and adventure seekers looking to conquer Mindanao's majestic mountain.

Crocodile Park

Crocodile Park is a popular wildlife attraction in Davao City, featuring crocodiles, exotic animals, and educational exhibits. It's perfect for families and curious visitors who want to explore nature up close.

Eden Nature Park

Eden Nature Park is a mountain resort surrounded by lush forests and cool climate. It offers outdoor activities, gardens, and scenic views â€” ideal for relaxation and eco-tourism.

Peoples Park

People's Park is a cultural-themed park in the heart of Davao City. It showcases local art, sculptures, and landscaped gardens, making it a peaceful spot for walking, sightseeing, and learning about Davao's heritage.

Philippine Eagle Center

The Philippine Eagle Center is a conservation sanctuary dedicated to the country's national bird. Visitors can witness the majestic Philippine Eagle and learn about efforts to protect endangered species.

Malagos Garden Resort

Malagos Garden Resort is a nature-themed park known for its beautiful gardens, bird shows, and chocolate museum. It's a great place for families and nature enthusiasts to explore and enjoy outdoor activities.

San Pedro Cathedral

The oldest church in Davao City, known for its unique architecture and religious significance.

Japanese Tunnel

A historical site from World War II, offering guided tours through the underground tunnels used by Japanese forces.

Magsaysay Park

A popular urban park featuring recreational facilities, gardens, and a peaceful environment for relaxation and family outings.
