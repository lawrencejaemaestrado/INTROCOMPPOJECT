Travel Tips

  • Respect local culture — dress modestly at heritage sites.
  • Try local food like Curacha, Satti, and Zamboanga-style Paella.
  • Learn basic Chavacano phrases like "Buenos dias" and "Gracias."
  • Book early during festivals or peak season.
  • Visit between November and May for best weather.
  • Help protect nature — don't litter or take seashells.
